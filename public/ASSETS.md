# Brand assets

## Logo

The site renders the logo from **`public/logo.svg`** (referenced by
`src/components/Logo.tsx` and the Open Graph tags in `src/app/layout.tsx`).

To change the logo, replace `public/logo.svg` with your own artwork (keep the
same filename). A square (1:1) badge works best in the dark navy navbar.

If you'd rather use a PNG/JPG, drop it in this folder (e.g. `logo.png`) and
update the `src="/logo.svg"` references in `Logo.tsx` and `layout.tsx`.
